About Our Cotton Factory

Sustainable Cotton Production Since 1985

Our family-owned cotton factory has been producing premium quality cotton for over three decades. We combine traditional methods with modern technology to create sustainable, ethical cotton products.

Located in the heart of the cotton belt, our factory processes only the finest cotton fibers, ensuring superior comfort and durability in all our products.

Our Production Process

1

Cotton Harvesting

Our cotton is hand-picked at peak ripeness to ensure the highest quality fibers. We use sustainable harvesting methods that protect both workers and the environment.

2

Ginning & Cleaning

Using state-of-the-art ginning equipment, we gently separate cotton fibers from seeds while maintaining fiber length and quality. The cotton is thoroughly cleaned to remove all impurities.

3

Spinning & Weaving

Our skilled technicians oversee the spinning process where fibers are transformed into yarn, then woven into fabric with precision machinery that ensures consistent quality.
